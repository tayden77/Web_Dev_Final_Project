Choose Your Character's Equipment

In the world of Dungeons & Dragons, your character's equipment includes everything they carry, from weapons and armor to adventuring gear. This gear is essential for survival, combat, and exploring the fantasy realms.

• Class-Based Equipment: Each class comes with a standard set of equipment, typically including weapons, armor, and other useful items for your character's adventures.

• Background-Based Gear: Your character's background also contributes to their starting equipment, offering items that reflect their life before adventuring.

• Purchasing Equipment: Alternatively, you may opt to use a set amount of gold (based on your class) to purchase equipment. This option allows for more personalized gear choices to suit your character's needs and preferences.
