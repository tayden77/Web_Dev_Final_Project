Choose Your Character's Spells

A Spell is the magical effect that occurs when a character takes and manipulates the invisible strands of raw, magical energy present in the world of D&D. Each spell caster gains this ability depending on their class:

• for clerics and paladins, their deity bestows this power upon them

• for bards, they manipulate the magic present in words - thought to have uttered the world into existence

• for druids and rangers, nature and nature deities grant them this power

• for sorcerers, a phenomenal event has imbued them with innate magical abilities

• for wizards, knowledge bestows them the secrets of the magical universe

• and for warlocks, a pact with an otherworldly being opens the door to the ability of spell casting.
