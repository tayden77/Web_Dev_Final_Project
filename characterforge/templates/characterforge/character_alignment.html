Choose Your Character's Alignment

In Dungeons & Dragons, alignment is a key aspect of your character's identity. It represents their moral compass and ethical beliefs, influencing how they interact with the world and make decisions. Alignment is a combination of two factors: one that describes morality (good, evil, or neutral) and another that describes attitude (lawful, chaotic, or neutral).
