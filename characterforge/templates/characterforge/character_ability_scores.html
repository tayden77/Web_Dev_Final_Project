Choose Your Character's Ability Scores

In the realm of Dungeons & Dragons, ability scores are the foundation of your character's talents and potential. They define how strong, agile, intelligent, and charming your character is. Whether you're swinging a sword, concocting a potion, or persuading an ally, these scores shape every action and decision. Will your character be a muscular champion, a nimble acrobat, a brilliant tactician, or a charismatic leader? Distribute your points across Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma to mold a hero that aligns with your envisioned journey.

One way to distribute your points is called "Point-buy", where each score starts at 8 and you have a total of 27 points to distribute across the different ability scores to a maximum of 15 for each individual score.
